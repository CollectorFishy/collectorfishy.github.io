Locations.

When most people look at pixel art they think of video games. Yes, video games are a very common place to find pixel art but pixel art can be found in other places to. In the gallery, only two out of all of those photos are from video games. Pixel art is an style of art that people don't give enough respect to it compared to other styles of art. Although video games are the origin of pixel art.

About.

Pixel art is nothing other than a style of art work. Pixel art can be created by anyone as long as they've got the right tools.

Useful tools

You could use a ruler to make pixel art but if you have a computer I highly recomend you to just use that. Now photoshop is a very useful if you know how to use it. You would have to make one layer for every single pixel just incase you screw up. The good thing about using photoshop is that you can zoom in to each pixel make sure its perfect. Another easy way is to use the game called Minecraft. Minecraft is all in cubes. So if you build a flat surface in the game you can create some pixel art!
